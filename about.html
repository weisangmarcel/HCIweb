Papuma

Papuma, a very beautiful tourist spot. With a very awesome view. Combining the beauty of infinite oceans with well-preserved forests.

Papuma Which located in South Coast of Jawa Timur Province in Indonesia is one of many beach that has extraordinary scenery. With White sand that spread along the shore, it makes people more insterested to come to Papuma to relax themselves. Aside from the sea, Papuma also have a lot of endemics which spread across Papuma, such as lizard, chicked, Bird, and even Deer also exist in Papuma.

This white sandy beach with exotic views is located in the East Java region, more precisely the Jember area. The Fee to Enter the beach on a weekday Monday to Friday is IDR 7,000, while on Saturdays and Sundays it is IDR 15,000. With Many Accomodation such as Hotel and Restaurant, Tourist doesn't have to worry about where they can sleep and they can ejoy the beauty of sunset and sunrise in Papuma.
